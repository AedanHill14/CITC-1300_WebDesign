About Mass Effect

Mass Effect is a galaxy-spanning 3rd person shooter RPG. The series consists of 4 games, the first of which being released in 2007 and the most recent in 2017. Later, the original trilogy (Mass Effect 1-3) was re-released and remastered in 2021 with the release of the Mass Effect Legendary Edition. Mass Effect was first released by Bioware in 2007, a company owned by EA. The series remained an Xbox and PC exclusive up until the release of Mass Effect 3 in 2012, when it was ported to Playstation.

Throughout the years, Mass Effect has been praised for its phenomenal world builing, well written characters, and for the freedom given to the player through both customization and through weighted choices affecting the story across all 4 games.
