Current

Breaking Benjamin has 5 current menbers, Benjamin Burnley, Jasen Rauch, Kieth Wallen, Aaron Bruch, and Shaun Foist. The band's founder and namesake is Benjamin Burnley, the lead vocalist and guitarist since 1998. He is currently 47 years old and was born in Atlantic City, New Jersey. The band also has 2 other guitarists, Jasen Rauch and Keith Wallen. Jasen Rauch is the lead guitarist, and Kieth Wallen is a rhythm guitarist alongside Benjamin, and is also a backing vocalist. Aaron Bruch is the bassist, and is also a backing vocalist, and Shaun Foist is the drummer. Jasen Rauch, Keith Wallen, and Shaun Foist all joined Breaking Benjamin in 2014, replacing the older band members who were forced to leave due to legal crisis. Unfortunately, Shaun had to leave the band in 2025 to focus on his health.

Previous

Before the band's legal trouble in 2011, Aaron Fink was the lead guitarist, Mark James Klepaski was the bassist, and Chad Czeglia was the drummer. Unfortunately, due to legal trouble and personal issues, all members aside from the founder, Benjamin Burnley, were forced to leave.
